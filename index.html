A Study on Wealth and Contentment

Introduction

The following is a relatively small, local study on the relationaship between income and happiness. The analysis proposed here takes into consideration London boroughs only, showing the relation between income and general levels of happiness of people living in each borough.

The Bubble Chart visualisation below takes data from two separate datasets, each representing one of the metrics, and displays the correlation between those, for each borough.
